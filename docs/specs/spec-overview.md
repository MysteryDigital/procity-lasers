# Spec: Project Overview

**Status:** Draft — requirements in definition
**Last updated:** 2026-07-01

## 1. Summary

A new marketing website for **Pro City Laser Removal** (procitylasers.com), a
British company providing specialist laser cleaning and restoration services.
The company operates in a niche few UK competitors serve, and the site needs to
communicate that expertise credibly to a professional, procurement-driven
audience.

## 2. The Company

Pro City Laser Removal uses laser cleaning technology to deliver services that
are difficult, non-abrasive, and environmentally cleaner than traditional
methods (e.g. sandblasting or chemical stripping).

### Services

- **Graffiti removal**
- **Heritage conservation** — cleaning and restoration of historic stone,
  masonry, and monuments
- **Fire damage removal** — soot and char remediation
- **Industrial cleaning**
- **Paint removal / coating stripping**

## 3. Target Market

The audience is professional and institutional, not consumer. Buyers are
budget-holders and specifiers making procurement decisions.

- **Local councils** — public realm, heritage assets, anti-graffiti contracts
- **Insurance work** — fire and damage restoration claims
- Likely also: heritage bodies, conservation architects, facilities managers,
  main contractors

Implication: these buyers evaluate suppliers on **credibility, compliance, and
track record**, not price alone.

## 4. Goals

1. Establish Pro City as a credible, established specialist in a niche field.
2. Generate qualified enquiries from councils, insurers, and related buyers.
3. Communicate the breadth of services clearly and quickly.
4. Provide the trust signals professional buyers look for (case studies,
   accreditations, before/after evidence).

## 5. Brand & Design Direction

- **Clean, modern, professional** — reflecting the precision of laser technology
  and the seriousness of the audience.
- Suited to institutional buyers: trustworthy, understated, not gimmicky.
- Strong use of **before/after visuals** — the results are inherently visual and
  are the strongest sales tool.

## 6. Scope (to be confirmed)

- Case studies / project gallery with before/after imagery

### To be decided
